Bradley Bazzle is a writer in Athens, Georgia.  His stories appear in New England Review, New Ohio Review, Epoch, Phoebe, Web Conjunctions, Bad Penny Review, Beloit Fiction Journal, and elsewhere, and have won awards from Third Coast and The Iowa Review.  Bradley grew up in Dallas, Texas, and has an MFA from Indiana University.  In Athens, he teaches writing at the University of Georgia and does improvisational comedy with Improv Athens.  Other hobbies including backpacking with his wife, growing sweet potatoes, and tinkering with Von Lessinger equipment.
