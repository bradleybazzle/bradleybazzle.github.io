Bradley Bazzle grew up in Dallas, Texas, and has an MFA from Indiana University.  Now he lives in Athens, Georgia, where he teaches writing at the University of Georgia and does improvisational comedy with Improv Athens.  Other hobbies including backpacking and growing sweet potatoes.  Bradley's stories appear in New England Review, New Ohio Review, Epoch, Phoebe, Web Conjunctions, Bad Penny Review, Beloit Fiction Journal, and elsewhere, and have won awards from Third Coast and The Iowa Review.
