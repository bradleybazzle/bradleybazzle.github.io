Bradley Bazzle

Bradley is a writer and teacher in Athens, Georgia. His short stories have appeared in The Iowa Review, New England Review, New Ohio Review, Epoch, and elsewhere. His writing has been reprinted in Fiction Daily, nominated for several Pushcarts, received the 2011 Tim R. McGinnis Award for humor from The Iowa Review, and was runner-up in the 2013 New Ohio Review fiction contest, judged by Stuart Dybek.
